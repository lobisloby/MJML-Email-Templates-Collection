Lowtide
A walk along the wind-swept seashore inspired the creation of the Lowtide coffee table by Dutch designer Roderick Vos. The piece uses a computer-guided milling technique to give the top a ‘handcrafted’ pattern, that resembles the sand structures left on the beach at low tide.⁠
